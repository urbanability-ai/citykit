# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors, regardless of background or experience.

## Expected Behavior

- **Be respectful:** Treat others with courtesy and kindness
- **Be constructive:** Offer helpful feedback; assume good intent
- **Be inclusive:** Welcome people of all backgrounds and levels of expertise
- **Give credit:** Acknowledge the contributions of others

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hostile language
- Intimidation or personal attacks
- Unwelcome sexual comments or advances
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Any behavior that creates an unsafe or exclusionary environment

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **Report to maintainers:** Open a private issue or contact a project maintainer
2. **Be specific:** Describe what happened, when, and who was involved (if comfortable)
3. **Confidentiality:** Reports will be handled confidentially

## Enforcement

Maintainers have the right to remove content, comments, or contributions that violate this code. Repeated violations may result in removal from the project.
